What Is Freedom To You?

Written on August 5th, 2023 by Roman Freeman

What is Freedom, actually?

Of course there are vocabulary definitions and all kinds of biases, you might think money means freedom for example. But does it really? I wanted to find out what does freedom really mean to people that live around me.

That’s why I hit the streets of Potsdam, Germany, to find out what people think.

I was surprised to get a wide variety of answers from famous quotes to simple but very much to the point answers. I hope you get something out of it.

As a bonus I’ve added subtitles, so you can follow along and learn some German or English this way too!
